About fashionCORE

Reimagining the way people shop for clothes using AI technology

Our Story

Founded in 2020, fashionCORE was born out of a simple observation: people love shopping for clothes online but struggle with the uncertainty of how they'll look in them. Our founder, Kaushal Shah, experienced this frustration firsthand while managing his family's traditional garment business in India.

With a background in both fashion and technology, Kaushal assembled a team of AI experts and fashion industry veterans to develop a solution. After two years of research and development, we launched our first virtual try-on platform, focusing initially on traditional Indian wear.

Today, fashionCORE serves thousands of customers and businesses worldwide, helping them make more confident fashion decisions through our innovative AI-powered technology.

Our Vision

At fashionCORE, we envision a future where the boundary between online and offline shopping disappears. A world where anyone can confidently purchase clothing online, knowing exactly how it will look on them.

We're working towards a seamless shopping experience where virtual try-on is an integral part of every fashion purchase, reducing returns, increasing customer satisfaction, and making fashion more accessible to everyone.

Our technology is continuously evolving, with ongoing research in AI, computer vision, and 3D modeling to create increasingly realistic and personalized virtual try-on experiences.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the fashion shopping experience by leveraging AI technology, making it more personalized, convenient, and sustainable for both consumers and businesses.
