1. Introduction

tryontrend ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at tryontrend.com, including any other media form, media channel, mobile website, or mobile application related or connected thereto (collectively, the "Site").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our Site, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is not to use our Site.

This Privacy Policy applies to information we collect:

  • On our Site
  • In email, text, and other electronic messages between you and our Site
  • Through our virtual try-on service and other interactive features on our Site
  • When you interact with our advertising and applications on third-party websites and services

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, information we collect automatically when you use our Site, and information from third-party sources.

Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, fill out a form, make a purchase, upload images for virtual try-on, communicate with us, or otherwise interact with our Site. This information may include:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, postal address, phone number, username, and password
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, and other payment information. Note: we do not store your full credit card details on our servers.
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, preferences, and other information you choose to add to your account
  • User Content: Photos, including images of yourself that you upload to use our virtual try-on service, product selections, and other content you submit through our Site
  • Communication Data: Information you provide when you contact us with inquiries, customer support requests, or feedback

Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device and interactions with our Site. This information includes:

  • Device Information: Your IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • Usage Information: Pages or screens you view, time spent on our Site, navigation paths, and features you use
  • Location Information: General location information derived from your IP address
  • Log Information: Information our servers automatically collect when you access our Site, such as your browser type, access times, and pages viewed
  • Virtual Try-On Data: Technical data related to your virtual try-on sessions, including garment selections, processed image outputs, and technical metadata

Information from Third-Party Sources

We may receive information about you from third-party sources, such as:

  • Social media platforms when you connect your account with our Site
  • Business partners, such as payment processors and authentication services
  • Marketing partners and analytics providers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services: Including to process transactions, manage your account, provide customer support, and enable the virtual try-on functionality
  • Personalize your experience: Customize content, recommendations, and marketing based on your preferences and interactions
  • Process payments: Complete purchases, process refunds, and prevent fraudulent transactions
  • Communicate with you: Send service-related notices, marketing communications, respond to your comments and questions, and provide customer service
  • Improve our algorithms: Enhance our virtual try-on technology and other features
  • Research and development: Develop new products and services and analyze the effectiveness of our Site
  • Enforce our terms, conditions, and policies: Protect our legal rights and comply with legal obligations
  • Marketing and advertising: Display relevant advertisements and promotions on and off our Site and measure their effectiveness

5. Data Security and Storage

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When determining how long to keep your information, we consider:

  • The amount, nature, and sensitivity of the personal information
  • The potential risk of harm from unauthorized use or disclosure
  • Whether we can achieve our purposes through other means
  • Legal, regulatory, accounting, or reporting requirements

Treatment of Virtual Try-On Images

We understand the sensitive nature of the personal photos you upload for our virtual try-on service. Your privacy and security are our priority with regard to these images:

  • Your uploaded photos are processed securely on our servers to provide the virtual try-on functionality
  • Original uploaded photos are retained for 30 days after your try-on session for troubleshooting purposes, after which they are automatically deleted
  • Generated try-on results may be stored in your account history if you're a registered user
  • We do not use your personal photos for purposes other than providing the virtual try-on service without your explicit consent
  • We implement additional security measures for image storage and processing systems

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We and our service providers use various technologies to collect information automatically when you access and use our Site, including cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the Site to function properly
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our Site
  • Marketing Cookies: Allow us to deliver relevant advertisements

Your Cookie Choices

Most web browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or delete certain cookies. However, if you disable cookies, you may not be able to use all the features of our Site.

Do Not Track

Some browsers have "Do Not Track" features that allow you to tell websites not to track you. We currently do not respond to Do Not Track signals because there is not yet a common understanding of how to interpret these signals.
